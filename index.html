My Coding Journey

As far back as I can remember I've been a passionate creator, problem solver and learner of new skills.

Like many school age children I was passionate about video games, and my first experience with HTML was creating a fan site for my favorite game at the time. Another one of my games had a level editor and I remember spending hours designing maps on paper and reverse-engineering the programming interface through tutorial maps.

At fifteen I developed a passion for guitar, and this led me to writing songs, earning a music degree, teaching professionally, and starting my own business with many contract teachers under my banner.

As a musician I developed a growth mindset, and to always be learning. I learned firsthand that I cannot master a piece of music in one all-night practice session, but that I can reshape my brain and finger dexterity with daily practice. As a coder, this mindset drives me to code every day.

Although my business has been successful, I grew restless to develop hard skills. Browsing through a local bookstore I found myself in the tech section, looking at manuals for HTML, CSS and JavaScript. My early experience with HTML and styles led me to wonder "What is JavaScript?" I quickly got hooked, learning on free and affordable resources the internet.

I used as much free time as I could toiling away learning code on my own. I told my wife it was a backup plan in case something happened to the business, hesitant to reveal that it was my dream to make it my ultimate career.

One day, my wife and decided to find a new home for Sadie, one of our dogs who had many special needs. It was a difficult and painful decision, that lead to some emotional moments. My wife wanted me to be happy and asked me: "What would make you happy?"

Being asked this question provided clarity. I said: "I really want to enroll in a coding bootcamp and launch a new career in coding." I knew this decision may be risky would require her full support.

Since starting and completing Austin Coding Academy, my passion for coding has only grown. I especially like diving deep into javascript datastructures and algorithms.

I would be excited to work with a collaborative team to create exceptional user experience, solve problems and learn new skills along the way!
